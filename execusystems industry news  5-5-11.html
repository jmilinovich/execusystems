 Study: Shoppers reward good service with higher spending
 Consumers tend to spend more with merchants that provide good customer service, according to a new study from American Express. About 70% of U.S. consumers are willing to spend an average of 13% more to reward good service; the survey also found that 60% consumers don't believe most merchants provide the kind of service they're seeking.
